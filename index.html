Know about Cafe Fare What is Cafe Fare? CafeFare is a startup that Rohan Dafare & Pranjali Musale have founded, which provides a web-based application that enables cafes and restaurants to receive orders directly from customers' phones by scanning a QR code on the table. The app streamlines the ordering process, making it more convenient and efficient for customers and staff. The app also provides real-time updates on the status of the order, allowing customers to track their food's progress and estimated time of delivery. CafeFare is a web-based application that utilizes QR codes to enable customers to order food and drinks directly from their table. The process is simple and efficient, with the app designed to reduce wait times, increase accuracy, and provide a seamless dining experience. To use CafeFare, customers scan the QR code located on their table using their smartphone camera. This action takes them to the CafeFare website, where they can view the menu, place their order, and select any customizations or add-ons. The app can handle multiple orders, allowing customers to place orders for the entire table. Once the order is placed, the CafeFare system sends a notification to the kitchen, alerting staff to the new order. The app also provides real-time updates on the status of the order, letting customers know when their food or drinks are being prepared, when they are ready for pickup, and when they have been served. The app's intuitive interface makes it easy for customers to customize their orders, including specifying allergies or dietary restrictions. Overall, CafeFare's use of QR codes and web-based technology provides a convenient, efficient, and safe way for customers to order and enjoy their dining experience.
